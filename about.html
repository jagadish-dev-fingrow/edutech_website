Who We Are

Our Story

MN Technologies Skill Development was founded with one clear focus: to make quality, practical IT training accessible to everyone — freshers, non-IT professionals, and career switchers looking to break into tech.

Based in Marathahalli, Bangalore — one of India's biggest IT hubs — we saw a persistent problem: students were coming out of colleges and other training centers knowing theory, but struggling to do actual work.

So we built a different kind of training. Every class uses real scenarios. Every session focuses on what's actually asked at interviews. And every student gets personal attention — our batches stay at 10–20 students deliberately.

Our Mission

To make students job-ready with real-world skills — not just theoretical knowledge. Every student who walks out of MN Technologies should be able to work on day one, answer interview questions confidently, and grow in their career.

Our Vision

To build a strong IT career foundation for every learner — regardless of their background. We believe talent is everywhere. Our job is to turn that talent into skills that the market recognizes and rewards.
